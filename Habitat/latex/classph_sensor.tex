Starts the sleepmode of the ph-\/module. 

To conserve energy in between readings, the ph-\/module can be put into a low power sleep state. This will turn off the L\+E\+Ds and shut down almost all of the internal workings. The power consumption will be reduced to 1.\+16 m\+A at 5\+V and 0.\+995 m\+A at 3.\+3\+V. To wake the ph-\/ module, send it any command. After the device is woken up, 4 consecutive readings should be taken before the readings are considered valid.
