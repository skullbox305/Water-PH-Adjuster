bool phSensor::startSleepmode ( )

Starts the sleepmode of the ph-module.

To conserve energy in between readings, the ph-module can be put into a low power sleep state. This will turn off the LEDs and shut down almost all of the internal workings. The power consumption will be reduced to 1.16 mA at 5V and 0.995 mA at 3.3V. To wake the ph- module, send it any command. After the device is woken up, 4 consecutive readings should be taken before the readings are considered valid.

Returns
true if it succeeds, false if it fails.
